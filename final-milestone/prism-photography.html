PRISM PHOTOGRAPHY

What is Prism Photography?

Prism photography is a type of photography that captures a subject using a glass prism or another glass object. This type of photography is very simple and can easily be done if you have a glass prism or object. Depending on how close you hold the prism and the position of the object, you can get a totally different photograph.

The Prism

A prism is a transparent item that refracts light. It usually has two sides that have an angle between them. When light goes through the object, it refracts the light. This normally creates a rainbow effect. It can also create other natural effects. Instead of using filters avalible on a phone, this is a fun way to create your own "filters."
