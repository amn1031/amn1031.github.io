KINETIC PHOTOGRAPHY

What is Kinetic Photography?

Kinetic photography is a type of photography that captures movement. It is very abstract and could be considered motion blurred in some cases. This is an experimental type of photography and can be risky due to camera tossing.

Camera Tossing

Camera tossing can be very risky, but creates some of the best kinetic photographs. Ryan Gallagher improved this technique by adjusting the shutter speed. Which made it so you could potentally create circles, write names, or create patterns.
