ASTROPHOTOGRAPHY

What is Astrophotography?

Astrophotography is a type of photography that captures objects in space. It is also used to capture events that happen in space. These photographs are often used by scientists to observe and find new objects in space.

Find Out More Information Here!

A Telescope's Role

Telescopes are often used with tripods to capture the objects or events in space. Without connecting a camera to a telescope the images are not as magnified and clear. It also makes it so long exposures are steady, which makes the photographs clear.
